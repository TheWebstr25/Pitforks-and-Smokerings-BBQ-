About Us

Located right off Highway 84 in Slaton, Texas, Pitforks & Smoke Rings BBQ has become a road-trip must-stop for smoky, slow cooked brisket, ribs, and southern style comfort food. No reservations needed, just good food, friendly faces, and that unmistakable Texas pit aroma.
